# Android

> The Android ecosystem's APIs and services for building apps across phones, tablets, wearables, TVs, cars, and XR — spanning the Android platform framework, Jetpack, Google Play services, Firebase, Google Maps, ML Kit, on-device Gemini Nano AI, and the Google Play Developer publishing API. This llms.txt is generated by API Evangelist from the catalog in apis.yml; no official /llms.txt is published on developer.android.com (probed 404 on 2026-06-20).

## APIs

- [Android Platform APIs](https://developer.android.com/reference): Core Android framework APIs for building Android applications.
- [Google Play Services APIs](https://developers.google.com/android/guides/overview): APIs for integrating Google services into Android apps.
- [Firebase Android APIs](https://firebase.google.com/docs/android/setup): Firebase SDKs and APIs for Android app development.
- [Google Maps Android API](https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/android-sdk): Maps, location, and geospatial data for Android apps.
- [Android Jetpack APIs](https://developer.android.com/jetpack): Libraries to help developers follow best practices.
- [Google Play Developer API](https://developer.android.com/google/play/developer-api): REST APIs for publishing, reporting, purchases, subscriptions, reviews, and orders.
- [Google Play Billing API](https://developer.android.com/google/play/billing): In-app purchases and subscriptions.
- [Android NDK APIs](https://developer.android.com/ndk): Native (C/C++) development kit.
- [Google ML Kit Android APIs](https://developers.google.com/ml-kit): On-device machine learning (text, face, barcode, image labeling).
- [Android Health Connect API](https://developer.android.com/health-and-fitness/health-connect): Consolidated health and fitness data platform.
- [Android CameraX API](https://developer.android.com/media/camera/camerax): Jetpack camera library.
- [Wear OS APIs](https://developer.android.com/wear): Smartwatch and wearable apps.
- [Android for Cars APIs](https://developer.android.com/cars): Android Auto and Android Automotive OS.
- [Google AdMob Android API](https://developers.google.com/admob): In-app advertising and monetization.
- [Android Accessibility APIs](https://developer.android.com/guide/topics/ui/accessibility): Accessible apps and accessibility services.
- [Android TV APIs](https://developer.android.com/tv): TV apps with Compose for TV and Leanback.
- [Google Play Integrity API](https://developer.android.com/google/play/integrity): Verify genuine apps and devices.
- [Android Credential Manager API](https://developer.android.com/identity/credential-manager): Passkeys, passwords, and federated sign-in.
- [Gemini Nano On-Device AI API](https://developer.android.com/ai/gemini-nano): On-device generative AI.
- [Gemini Developer API for Android](https://developer.android.com/ai/gemini/developer-api): Cloud Gemini for Android apps.
