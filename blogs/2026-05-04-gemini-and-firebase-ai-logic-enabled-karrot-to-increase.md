Posted by Thomas Ezan, Sr Developer Relations Engineer and Tracy Agyemang, Product Marketing ManagerKarrot is a hyperlocal, community-driven peer-to-peer marketplace app that enables users to buy, sell, and trade items with other verified users. Since launching in South Korea in 2015, the platform has expanded into global markets, amassing over 43 million registered users. After launching in North America, engineers at Karrot observed that 30% of users in the region use a non-English device language, such as Spanish.
