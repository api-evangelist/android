Posted by David Chou, Product Manager and Caren Chang, Developer Relations Engineer At Google, we’re committed to bringing the most capable AI models directly to the Android devices in your pocket. Today, we’re thrilled to announce the release of our latest state-of-the-art open model: Gemma 4 . These models are the foundation for the next generation of Gemini Nano, so code you write today for Gemma 4 will automatically work on Gemini Nano 4-enabled devices that will be available later this year.
