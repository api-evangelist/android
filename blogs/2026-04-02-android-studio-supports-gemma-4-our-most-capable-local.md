Posted by Matthew Warner, Google Product Manager Every developer's AI workflow and needs are unique, and it's important to be able to choose how AI helps your development. In January, we introduced the ability to choose any local or remote AI model to power AI functionality in Android Studio, and today, we're announcing the availability of Gemma 4 for AI coding assistance in Android Studio. This new local model trained on Android development provides the best of both worlds: the privacy and cost-efficiency of on-device processing alongside state-of-the-art reasoning and tool-calling…
