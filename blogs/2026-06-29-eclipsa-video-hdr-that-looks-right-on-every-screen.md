Google introduces Eclipsa Video, a new HDR standard built on the open SMPTE ST 2094-50 specification developed with Apple and NBCUniversal, giving compatible displays precise frame-by-frame instructions to render HDR consistently. The feature launches natively in Android 17 with support built into Jetpack Media3 for developers implementing playback and capture.
