Posted by Andrew Lewis, Software Engineer Media3 1.10 is out! Media3 1.10 includes new features, bug fixes and feature improvements, including Material3-based playback widgets, expanded format support in ExoPlayer and improved speed adjustment when exporting media with Transformer. Read on to find out more, and check out the full release notes for a comprehensive list of changes.
