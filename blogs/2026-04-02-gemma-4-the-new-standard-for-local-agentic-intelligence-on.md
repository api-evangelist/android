Posted by Matthew McCullough, VP of Product Management Android Development Today, we are enhancing Android development with Gemma 4 , our latest state-of-the-art open model designed with complex reasoning and autonomous tool-calling capabilities. Our vision is to enable local agentic AI on Android across the entire software lifecycle, from development to production. Android supports a range of Gemma 4 models, from the most efficient ones running directly on-device in your apps to more powerful ones running on your development machine to help you build apps.
