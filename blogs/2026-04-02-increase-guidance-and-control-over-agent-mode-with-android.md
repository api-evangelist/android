Posted by Matt Dyor, Senior Product Manager Android Studio Panda 3 is now stable and ready for you to use in production. This release gives you even more control and customization over your AI-powered workflows, making it easier than ever to build high-quality Android apps. Whether you're bringing new capabilities to an existing app or standing up a brand new app, these updates elevate your development experience by allowing your AI Agent in Android Studio to learn your specific practices and giving you granular control over its permissions.
